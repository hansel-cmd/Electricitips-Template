About Electricitips

What is ElectriciTips?

A Web Application that records the rated power input of all appliances used in the household and provides an estimated monthly and daily electricity usage and cost.

What is its goal?

Aims to bridge the gap of the lack of tools for managing energy resources with an application that mainly features real-time measurement of energy consumption of appliances provided with estimated bill in the end of the month of usage.

Concerns on Global Warming

The Bigger Scope

Coal power plants emit huge amounts of greenhouse gas that contribute to global warming.

The Narrow Scope

The increase of carbon emissions brought about by increase of electricity demand by households has led further to global warming which results in climate change.

How does the app work?

It's as easy as a few clicks...

ElectriciTips records each power rating you enter and calculates its consumption based on the duration it is used.

The electricity consumed in total is multiplied by the cost per kWh.

The average daily usage is used to calculate your estimated monthly usage.
